Project intake for scope review and proposal preparation

This intake is designed to help EcoViva understand your project, define the right scope and prepare the right next step or proposal for your selected service.

Why this intake matters

We use this intake to understand what you are looking for, assess the project direction and determine what is needed before moving to the next stage.

What happens next

Depending on the request, the next step may include an internal review, a clarification call, a site visit and the preparation of a tailored proposal.

What this intake is for

This is not a generic contact form. It is the starting point for project understanding, scope definition and offer preparation.
