EcoViva Project Intake

Start with clarity before we prepare your proposal.

This intake is designed to understand your project, define the right scope and prepare a tailored proposal for the selected service. Whether you are planning renovation, insulation, solar or technical advisory support, this is the first step to structure the project properly.

What this intake is for

To map your project, understand what you are looking for and determine what we need internally to prepare the right next step.

What this intake is not

This is not a generic contact form. It is the starting point for project review, proposal preparation and the advisory process around your chosen service.

How the intake process works

The exact path depends on your project, but the intake generally follows a clear internal process from first request to proposal and planning.

1

Submit your intake

You tell us what you are looking for so we can understand the project direction and requested service.

2

Internal review

We review the request internally and determine what is needed to move the project forward correctly.

3

Follow-up call

We contact you to clarify the request, align expectations and confirm the right next step.

4

Site visit when needed

For certain projects, a site visit is needed to assess the technical situation and define the scope properly.

5

Proposal preparation

We prepare the proposal based on the selected service, the project details and the outcome of the review process.

6

Proposal discussion

We review the offer together during a site or office meeting, explain the scope and adjust where needed.

7

Planning the works

Once approved, we move into the preparation and planning phase of the project or works.

Not every project follows every step in the exact same way, but this gives you a clear idea of how EcoViva structures the intake and proposal process.
